Our global statement of energy conservation is:
$$\dfrac{dU_q}{dt} + \dfrac{dU_e}{dt} = -\iint \mathbf{S}\cdot d\mathbf{A}$$
Which term describes that energy of the electromagnetic field?

1. $\frac{dU_q}{dt}$
2. $\frac{dU_e}{dt}$
3. $-\iint \mathbf{S}\cdot d\mathbf{A}$
4. ???

Note:
* Correct Answer: B
</section>
